Project Overview

As the AI Architect at Coupang, I led the enterprise-wide transformation that fundamentally changed how our engineering organization builds software. This wasn't just about deploying AI tools—it was about creating a comprehensive ecosystem that made AI a natural extension of every engineer's workflow.

  • Timeline: 2024 - Present
  • Location: Mountain View, CA
  • Role: AI Architect & Technical Lead
  • Team Size: Cross-functional team of 15+ engineers

Key Technical Implementations

1. AI IDE Deployment (Windsurf)

Rolled out Windsurf AI IDE to our entire engineering organization, achieving unprecedented adoption rates through careful change management and technical integration.

  • Deployed to 2,500+ engineers across all teams
  • 90%+ adoption rate within 6 months
  • Custom integrations with internal development environment
  • Automated setup and configuration management

2. Centralized MCP Gateway

Built a secure, audited gateway for AI tool access, ensuring enterprise-grade security while maintaining developer experience.

  • TypeScript-based proxy with full audit logging
  • OAuth integration with corporate SSO
  • Sub-50ms latency overhead
  • 99.99% availability SLA
  • Automated rate limiting and cost controls

3. AI Observability Platform

Created comprehensive observability infrastructure to measure AI impact and guide organizational adoption.

  • Processes 1M+ LLM requests daily
  • Real-time token usage and cost tracking
  • Productivity metrics and quality indicators
  • Security monitoring and anomaly detection
  • Custom dashboards for different stakeholders

4. Emux (Engineer Multiplexer)

Developed an agent orchestration platform that manages complex multi-step AI workflows across our engineering systems.

  • Handles 10,000+ automated workflows daily
  • Integrates with Slack, CI/CD, and internal systems
  • Custom DSL for workflow definition
  • Self-healing capabilities and error recovery

5. Work-as-Code Framework

Transformed manual engineering operations into programmatic interfaces accessible by AI agents.

  • Created 50+ micro-MCP servers
  • Standardized interfaces for common operations
  • Enabled AI-driven automation of routine tasks
  • Reduced context switching by 40%
